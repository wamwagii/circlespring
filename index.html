About Our School

Discover the Circle Spring difference in Salama Estate, Thika

Established in 2005, Circle Spring School has been at the forefront of providing quality education in Salama Estate, Thika for over 18 years. Our mission is to cultivate intellectual curiosity, foster creativity, and develop responsible global citizens.

Our campus, located in the heart of Salama Estate, offers state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, science laboratories, a library, sports fields, and dedicated art and music studios.

Everything you need to know about Circle Spring School in Salama Estate, Thika

Our admissions process involves: 1) Submitting an inquiry form, 2) Scheduling a campus tour, 3) Completing the application, 4) Student assessment, and 5) Final enrollment. The entire process typically takes 2-3 weeks.

Our fees cover tuition, textbooks, basic stationery, and access to all school facilities. Additional costs may include uniforms, specialized extracurricular activities, and field trips.

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), and cash payments. Fees can be paid annually, per term (3 terms), or through monthly installment plans with a small administrative fee.
